About Me

Amira Khattap is an English lecturer and linguistics specialist, graduated from the Faculty of Arts and Humanities, Department of English, Class of 2021 — Suez Canal University. She currently works as an Arabic and Quran Tutor at CU Academy and is passionate about empowering learners to express themselves confidently. Amira believes that language learning is a bridge between cultures and a tool for human connection.
